About me

Autobiography

My name is Yon-Gjun Hou and I am now a senior studying in EE, Electrical Engineering. Using one sentence to describe my personality, "Never regret the consequence of what you did, but regret why you never did." I’m an extroverted person in private life, so there’s no chance of getting to know me, if you don’t start a conversation.

I am currently collaborating with other students to work on an independent study, and the theme is "Reconfigurable Modular Robot Motion Control”. We are divided into hardware and software groups supervised by Dr. Wei-Yu Chiu. The software team is responsible for the development of algorithms, using genetic algorithms to select genes and hybridize the excellent genes to get a better next generation. I am a member of the hardware team. Our team is responsible for tuning modular robots and make the hardware sensors learn the surrounding environment.


If you want to know more about me, the following are my experiences.
  1. National Tsinghua University Academic Excellence Award of the Department of Electrical Engineering in the second semester of 2018
  2. Second place in National Tsinghua University Independent Study of the Department of Electrical Engineering in the second semester of 2019
  3. Scholarship for Excellent Student of the Department of Electrical Engineering in National Tsinghua University
  4. Waker International Volunteer Certificate to Shaanxi, China
  5. Referee and level designer of Intelligent Ironman Creativity Contest organized by the ministry of Education, Taiwan
  6. Intern of National Space Organization, Taiwan

自傳

我的名字叫侯永駿，目前就讀電機工程學系。若用一句話來形容我的性格，“永遠不要後悔你所做的一切，而後悔為什麼你從未做過。”我對不熟稔的人較為冷漠，但私底下是一個外向的人，所以想認識我的話，主動交談會是最快且便捷的一條路。

我們的專題研究是由邱偉育指導，主題是“可重構模塊化機器人運動控制”分為硬體組和軟體組。軟體組負責演算法的開發，並使用基因演算法選擇基因並對其進行雜交，造出更優秀的子代。我是硬體組的成員，而我們的團隊負責調整模塊化機器人並使傳感器了解周圍的環境。
